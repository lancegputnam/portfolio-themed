Jackson and Perkins Mobile Interface

In the fall of 2014, I sat down to begin re-designing the mobile interface for Jackson and Perkins. With the holiday season quickly approaching, Jackson and Perkins mobile needed major design updates throughout the site. The mobile site had many problems, from inconsistent text sizes and colors to odd image layouts that did not best utilize the mobile screen real estate. After designing the mockups, work began on the mobile site overhaul. The majority of the work was completed in five phases. Restructuring the global header and footer was the first step, followed by redesigns to the home page layout, category views, and product pages. The new mobile design made better use of the valuable screen real estate and has a consistent design throughout. As a result of this redesign to the interface, mobile conversion rates vastly improved.
