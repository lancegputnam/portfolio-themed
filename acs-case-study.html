American Cancer Society Slideshow

For the past few years, I have had the pleasure of volunteering with Moonlight and Magnolias, an event benefitting the American Cancer Society. Hundreds of individuals attend the event each year to help find a cure to this disease. For the 2014 gala, I was asked to create a slideshow that would play throughout the event. The committee was looking for a stronger way to speak to all that the American Cancer Society does. I designed each slide and compiled them together, along with official videos from the American Cancer Society, for this slideshow.
