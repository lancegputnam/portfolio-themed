Kinloch Interiors

Kinloch Interiors is a young interior design firm based out of Greenville, South Carolina. Concentrating on residential remodeling, commercial design and interior decor, Kinloch Interiors needed a website with a portfolio that spoke to the strong design work and varied projects of the firm. A modern, clean and responsive web presence was designed and built for this full service firm.

Discover Kinloch Interiors

Modern and Responsive

Kinloch Interiors focuses on transitional decor and design, and I wanted the website to follow the same transitional aesthetic. While many of the designs by Kinloch Interiors feature a bright color palette, the colors of the website were kept minimal, so the imagery could speak for itself. The site is also fully responsive and optimized for a mobile-viewing experience.

Client Engagement

Instead of including the traditional testimonials page, I decided to to make the testimonials specific to each project. This created a cleaner way to view and read the all-important client reviews. Kinloch Interiors also wanted to make sure that social media links played an important role in the website, so each project page includes a link to pin and post any images on the site.
