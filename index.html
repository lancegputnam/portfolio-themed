THE PROCESS

Here are a few things I can assist with

IDEA CONSULTATION

The first step is to develop your idea into a well formed product. We will discuss your idea, who your target is and how we will win users over with your product.

USER EXPERIENCE

Once the idea takes shape, I will create wire frames and detailed mock ups of your new site or application. The UX process involves understanding how users search, browse and buy without getting lost.

USER INTERFACE

With the plans in place, the next step will be to design an attractive, modern, and responsive interface that users will want to keep coming back to.

DEVELOPMENT

When the UX and UI designs are completed, the coding of your project will begin. As a junior developer, I am able to develop many simple applications and for complex projects, I have some of the smartest resources around me to help get your idea off the ground.
